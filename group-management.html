Group Management Explained

In this page we provide a set of slides explaining the interaction between the different entities involved in a testing Termite environment. More specifically, we focus on the formation and destruction of device groups, and base our explanation on a demo application called MsgSender which can be used to exchange messages between devices in the same WiFi P2P group. The idea is to show the interaction the developer must have with the Termite client, as well as the results of said interactions.
